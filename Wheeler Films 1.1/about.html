It started with a birthday.

Two boys, ages 8 and 10, wanted to give their dad something cool for his birthday. They thought of an idea. They wanted to make a movie. All they had to film with was a Nintendo 2DS. But they were determined. The finished product was one they were pround of, though it was not as good as the ones they would make in the future. They could only dream of the wild possibilties they would eventually make.

Ethan and Euan Wheeler are the Co-founders of Wheeler Films. In 2022 they created their hit movie, White Terror. Since then they have been developing new movies, and posting new Youtube videos. Wheeler Films is a Christian movie-making studio dedicated to making clean, family-friendly movies for the whole family to watch together.

Bios

Ethan Wheeler is the older of the Wheeler brothers. He enjoys acting in CYT North Idaho but for his movies prefers filming and editing. He loves strategy board games and video games. He also codes his own games. In his free time he plays with his pets, Mudgy and Artemis, bakes deserts, and thinks up new movie ideas. He hopes you enjoy Wheeler Film's movies and asks you to keep your eyes peeled for new movies!

Euan Wheeler is the younger of the brothers. He loves acting and playing boardgames. He has acted with CYT North Idaho and his church's christmas plays. He has been in many Wheeler Films Movies and has narrated a few as well. He is also the Assistant in White Terror. He wants to keep acting and hopes to see Wheeler Films grow. Glory to God!
